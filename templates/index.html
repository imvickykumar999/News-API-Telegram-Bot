Virtual-Mouse

Virtual-Mouse is a project which controls mouse arrow virtually. Mid-point of two green pointer is position of mouse arrow on desktop. When distance between two green pointer is zero left button of mouse is clicked, and released after it becomes non-zero. Also I have taken reference for this project from udemy.
